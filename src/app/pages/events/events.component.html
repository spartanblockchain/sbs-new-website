SPARTAN BLOCKCHAIN SOLUTIONS
COMMUNITY MEETUP 2020

Thursday, March 26th | Broad Art Lab - East Lansing, MI | 6:00PM - 9:00PM

On the evening of March 26th, Spartan Blockchain Solutions is hosting a Greater Lansing Blockchain community meetup at the Broad Art Lab on Grand River Avenue. The Spartan Blockchain Solutions Community Meetup gives attendees the opportunity to network with invited blockchain & fintech firms from around the Metro Detroit area to discuss the blossoming educational community and startup ecosystem centered around blockchain and its applications around the Midwest. Free admission to the event will be complemented by light food, drinks, and a live band.

SPARTAN BLOCKCHAIN INNOVATION COMPETITION 2020

Friday, March 27th | Gaynor Entrepreneurship Lab - East Lansing, MI | 8:30AM - 4:30PM

The Spartan Blockchain Innovation Competition brings together teams from leading academic institutions to compete in a thought-provoking blockchain pitch competition. The event schedule fosters innovation and collaboration through ample networking opportunities spread across technology spaces located around Michigan State's campus. Attendees will gain access to professionals representing enterprise blockchain industry leaders and pitch solutions leveraging blockchain technology to address society's rapidly changing needs. We are proud to provide an inclusive environment for students to interact with disruptive technologies and continue their development as leaders in the blockchain space.
